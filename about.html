Team

The work was carried out by researchers from the Universidad Autónoma de Manizales - UAM (Manizales, Colombia), the Universidad Adolfo Ibáñez - UAI (Santiago, Chile), and the Universidad Nacional de Colombia - UNAL Manizales Headquarters (Manizales, Colombia).

This research is part of the "Bioinformatics and Artificial Intelligence Team" of the UAM and the "Ethical, Responsible and Transparent Algorithms Project" of the UAI in collaboration with the BID Lab (the innovation laboratory of the IDB Group).
